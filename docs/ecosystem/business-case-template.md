## Risk Assessment

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Phase 0 fails to validate | Low | Low | Investment is capped at 4 weeks; clear go/no-go |
| Officer resistance | Medium | Medium | Involve officers from Phase 0; it's support, not replacement |
| Legislative change during rollout | Medium | Low | Encoding pipeline makes re-encoding fast |
| Security/privacy concerns | Low | High | Sovereign deployment; no PII in rule engine |
| Vendor lock-in | None | None | Open source; switch implementers freely |
