# GovOps Certification Program

Professional certifications for individuals and organizations working with GovOps.

---

## Individual Certifications

### GovOps Certified Officer (GCO)

**For**: Public servants who use GovOps for case processing

**Requirements**:
- Complete Officer Training (Track 1)
- Pass practical assessment: process 3 cases with correct decisions and rationale
- Demonstrate understanding of authority chain and audit requirements

**Validity**: 2 years (renewal by processing 20+ cases in production)

### GovOps Certified Encoder (GCE)

**For**: Legal analysts and policy experts who encode legislation into rules

**Requirements**:
- Complete Rule Encoding Training (Track 2)
- Encode 10 articles from real legislation with peer review
- Demonstrate handling of transitional provisions and cross-references
- Pass quality audit: zero citation errors in assessment

**Validity**: 2 years (renewal by encoding one new program)

### GovOps Certified Implementer (GCI)

**For**: Developers and consultants who deploy GovOps

**Requirements**:
- Complete Technical Deployment Training (Track 3)
- Deploy GovOps to a production-grade environment
- Add one complete jurisdiction (10+ rules, 4+ test cases, passing tests)
- Contribute the jurisdiction back to the open-source project

**Validity**: 2 years (renewal by completing one implementation engagement)

### GovOps Certified Architect (GCA)

**For**: Senior consultants and technical leads

**Requirements**:
- Hold GCI certification
- Lead one Phase 0-2 implementation engagement
- Document the engagement as a published case study
- Pass architecture review: system design for a multi-program deployment

**Validity**: 3 years

---

## Organization Certifications

### GovOps Recognized Implementer

**For**: Consulting firms and system integrators

**Requirements**:
- At least 2 staff with GCI certification
- At least 1 staff with GCE certification
- Complete one Phase 0-2 engagement
- Publish a case study (anonymized if needed)
- Commit to contributing jurisdiction data back to open source

**Benefits**:
- Listed on the GovOps partner directory
- Access to implementation support channel
- Early access to new features and jurisdictions
- Use of "GovOps Recognized Implementer" mark

### GovOps Certified Training Provider

**For**: Training organizations

**Requirements**:
- At least 1 instructor with GCA certification
- Deliver training using approved curriculum
- Maintain student satisfaction above 80%
- Annual curriculum review and update

---

## Certification Governance

- Certifications are administered by the GovOps project maintainers
- Assessment criteria are published and version-controlled
- All certifications are recorded in a public registry
- Appeals process available for failed assessments
- No certification fee during the founding period (first 2 years)

---

## Why Certification Matters

For **governments**: confidence that implementation partners understand the method, not just the technology.

For **consultants**: differentiation in the market; proof of competence in a growing domain.

For **individuals**: portable credential that demonstrates expertise in policy-driven service delivery.
